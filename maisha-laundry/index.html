❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know. Can't find your answer? WhatsApp us!

How long does it take? +
Same day service if picked up by 9 AM — back by evening. Express service always available. Standard turnaround is 24 hours for most services.
Do you pick up from anywhere in Kampala? +
Yes! Free pickup and delivery across all of Kampala — Ntinda, Kololo, Bugolobi, Najjera, Kira, Naalya, Makindye, Rubaga, and everywhere in between. No extra fee ever.
How do I pay? +
MTN Mobile Money, Airtel Money, or cash on delivery. No upfront payment — just pay when your clean clothes are delivered back to you.
What if my clothes get damaged? +
We take the utmost care with every garment. We are insured and will take full responsibility for any damage caused by our handling. For delicate items, please let us know in advance.
Do you clean traditional clothes like Gomesi and Kanzu? +
Absolutely! We specialize in traditional Ugandan attire including Gomesi, Kanzu, and other cultural garments, as well as wedding gowns and bridal wear.
Do I need to provide detergent? +
No! We use premium detergents, fabric softeners, and specialist cleaning products. Everything is included in the price — just bag up your clothes.
What is the Basket Wash package? +
Our most popular deal! Fill any standard laundry basket with mixed clothes (up to 5 kg) and we wash, dry, fold, and deliver everything for a flat 30,000/=. Perfect for families.
Are you open on weekends and public holidays? +
Yes! Every day — Monday through Sunday including all public holidays — from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM. Laundry doesn't take days off, and neither do we.
How do I schedule a pickup? +
Three ways: (1) Use the booking form above, (2) Call us on 0780 603561 or 0751 571256, or (3) WhatsApp us on 0780 603561. We're available daily 8 AM to 8 PM.
Can I get a quote before booking? +
Yes! Use the price estimator in the booking form above, or WhatsApp us on 0780 603561 for an instant quote. We always give transparent, fair pricing with no hidden fees.
