Who We Are

More Than a Laundry Service

Maisha Laundry and Dry Cleaners Ltd is a modern, customer-focused laundry service based in Kyebando, Kampala. We provide professional washing, dry cleaning, ironing, and shoe cleaning — all with free pickup and delivery across Kampala.

We believe clean clothes should not be a chore. Whether you're a busy professional, a parent with young children, or a student, we take laundry off your plate so you can focus on what matters.

Our Story

How It All Started

📅
Founded 2024

Born From a Simple Idea

Maisha Laundry was born from a simple idea: laundry should be easy. The founder noticed that many people in Kampala struggled to find time for washing, ironing, and dry cleaning — especially with demanding work schedules and family responsibilities.

What started as a small neighbourhood service in Kyebando has grown into a trusted laundry brand serving customers across Kampala. We've built our reputation one clean shirt at a time — through reliability, attention to detail, and genuine care.

Today, we proudly serve busy families, professionals, students, and small businesses. And we're just getting started.
